# Getting Started with Skala

Guides and quickstarts for integrating Skala products.

SKALA AI is an advanced AI-powered data labeling engine designed to streamline and automate the process of annotating and labeling various types of data. It is particularly tailored for corporate use, enabling businesses to efficiently prepare high-quality datasets for machine learning and AI applications.

Skala AI's mission is to accelerate the development of AI applications. To enable teams to make faster progress, we began with data – the foundation of all AI applications. Skala AI turns raw data into high-quality training data by combining machine learning powered pre-labeling and active tooling with varying levels and types of human review.
